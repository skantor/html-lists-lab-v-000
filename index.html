How to make grilled cheese

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry fro 2 minutes
  1. just in case
